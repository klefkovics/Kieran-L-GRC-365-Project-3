About Us

Our Origin

Box Con formed out of a group of friends coming together over a love of one thing: cardboard. Our founder believed that, when it comes down to it, cardboard is really all you need in life, and wanted a way for other, like-minded box-heads to congregate and bond over our love for cutouts, packages, and everything in between.

Our Mission

Box Con was formed out of a singular love, but encompasses many facets of the cardboard hobby. New innovations, creative projects, fan meetups, cardboard celebrities, and more can all be found right here at Box Con.
